**[Bryan Low](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/cs/people/lowkh/)** is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at the National University of Singapore and the Director of AI Research at AI Singapore. He obtained the B.Sc. (Hons.) and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from National University of Singapore, Singapore, in 2001 and 2002, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, in 2009. His research interests include probabilistic & automated machine learning, planning under uncertainty, and multi-agent/robot systems.
